Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in the spices and salt, cook until oil separates.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice!
